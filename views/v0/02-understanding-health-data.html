2. {{content_title}}

Whenever you visit an NHS service, care professionals must keep records about you. These may be electronic, paper or a mixture of both.

Your care records will contain information about the care you have received. This is important for health and social care professionals to use to make decisions about your individual care. It will include personally identifiable data such as your name, age and address.
