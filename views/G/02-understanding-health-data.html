2. Understanding health data

Whenever you visit an NHS service, care professionals must keep records about you. These may be electronic, paper or a mixture of both

Inside your record

Your medical records will contain information about the care you have received. This is really important for health and social care professionals. It provides access to your medical history and important details, like allergies and past operations. This information is used to make careful decisions about your care.

Your medical history

Your medical history will include both past and on-going treatments. It will also contain any previous x-rays and discharge notes. The medicine you take, test results and any conditions you have will all be available in your record. This will also include personally identifiable data such as your name, age and address.
